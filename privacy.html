Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-01

Introduction

Melo ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal project operated by an individual developer. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our application and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Clipboard History: Melo stores clipboard content locally on your device to provide clipboard history functionality. This data is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Color History: Color values you pick using the Color Picker are stored locally.
  • Custom Language Packs: Any custom translations you create are stored locally.

Automatically Collected Information

Melo does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services in the current App Store build.

How We Use Your Information

Melo uses local app data solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the application functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features

Data Storage

All data collected by Melo is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Using the app's clear history function
  • Uninstalling the application

Permissions We Request

Melo requires the following permissions to function:

  • Screen Recording: To capture screenshots and screen regions for annotation and OCR features
  • Accessibility: To provide keyboard shortcuts and automation features
  • Microphone: For voice-to-text transcription functionality
  • Speech Recognition: To convert audio to text

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties. The current App Store build does not upload local records to our server.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored locally
  • Delete your local data
  • Revoke any permission at any time through System Settings

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
