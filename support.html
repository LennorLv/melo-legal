Support

Reach out for help, bug reports, or feature requests.

Last updated: 2026-07-02

Melo is maintained by an independent developer. I usually reply within 3 business days, depending on the issue and current availability.

Common Troubleshooting

  • Permissions: If screenshot, OCR, clipboard paste, microphone, or speech features do not work, open macOS System Settings and check the related Privacy & Security permissions.
  • Restart after permission changes: After changing permissions, quit Melo completely and open it again.
  • Subscription or purchase issues: Make sure you are signed in with the Apple ID used for purchase. You can also try the App Store purchase restoration flow inside the app, if available.
  • Clipboard history: Clipboard data is stored locally on your Mac. Clearing history or uninstalling the app may remove local records.
